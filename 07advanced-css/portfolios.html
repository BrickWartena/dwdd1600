About Me

Welcome to my artwork!

What mediums I work with

  • Traditional Oil Paint
  • Watercolor
  • Digital Media
  • Polymer Clay Sculpture

What I suggest:

  1. Start with the fundamental drawings, pencil on paper, cubes and spheres.
  2. Draw what you see and not what you know is there!
  3. Add some color in whatever medium you can get your hands on.

How do I get better?

Focused Practice
Pick a specific subject and work on it by finding references!
Finished Projects
I doubt simple practicing is your end goal for your art journey, you have to push yourself to create full peices even if they're bad!
Get excited
What draws your attention in everyday life? Trees? Architecture? People? Use it to fuel your projects and practice!
